Hello folks, I'm Hendra, a lead Web Engineer at [Stockbit](https://stockbit.com), working with Social tribe to deliver the best user experiences for Stockbitors. 

In my career at Stockbit, here are my notable deliveries:
* Stockbit Academy, a free multimedia learning platform for people who wants to learn trading stocks, is delivered in Bahasa Indonesia. 
Unboxing Saham is a free in-depth review of stocks/company delivered that is easy to understand for newbies.
* Stockbit Personal chat and Group Chat is a chatting features for Stockbitors, which allow them to exchange information with text, GIFs, PDFs, Stream Posts, etc.
* Stockbit's Research is a feature to allow Stockbitors to gain knowledge about a specific company by combining Academy Lessons, Unboxing Saham Page, Live Events, and Snips.
* Stockbit PDF Viewer is a feature to allow Stockbitors to read PDF documents without leaving the page.

I'm interested in web technology, user interaction, and developer experiences. My lifetime goal is to democratize education in web development, so everyone interested in web development can retrieve the same education quality.

Apart of my responsibility, I'm crafting fun stuff like [Tebak Kata Silang](https://tebak-kata-silang.hendrasadewa.com/) and writing blog post about programming at my [Medium](https://hendrasadewa.medium.com/).
