Hello folks, I'm Hendra, a lead Web Engineer at [Stockbit](https://stockbit.com), working with Social tribe to deliver the best user experiences for Stockbitors.

In my career at Stockbit, here are my notable deliveries:

### Stockbit Academy

![stockbit academy](/images/sb-academy.png)

A free multimedia learning platform for people who wants to learn trading stocks, is delivered in Bahasa Indonesia.

### Stockbit Personal chat and Group Chat

![stockbit chat](/images/sb-chat.png)

A chatting features for Stockbitors, which allow them to exchange information with text, GIFs, PDFs, Stream Posts, etc.

### Stockbit Live events

![stockbit chat](/images/sb-events.png)

A live streaming page contains all stockbit and partners related events.

### Stockbit Unboxing Saham

![stockbit research](/images/sb-unboxing.png)

Unboxing Saham is a free in-depth review of stocks/company delivered that is easy to understand for newbies.

### Stockbit's Research

![stockbit research](/images/sb-research.png)

A feature to allow Stockbitors to gain knowledge about a specific company by combining Academy Lessons, Unboxing Saham Page, Live Events, and Snips.
